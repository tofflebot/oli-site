About

Triple Element Films is a small, independent production company specialising in land, air and water based film production and run by Oliver Sharpe.

After studying photography at Coventry University and Documentary Film in Karlstad, Sweden, Oliver spent his formative career with duckrabbit, an award winning film production company.

After spending two years as a SCUBA Instructor and underwater cameraman in Tenerife he came back to the UK and set up Triple Element Films.

Combining the years of experience to offer quality media production in the air, on the land and underwater.
